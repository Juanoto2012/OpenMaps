OpenMaps is a next-generation mapping application that uses vector data to provide a smooth experience.

Key Features:
- Offline Navigation: Download maps and travel without an internet connection.
- Total Privacy: No trackers, no ads, and no telemetry.
- POIs: Advanced search for points of interest.
- Customization: Support for multiple layers and map styles.

Version 1.0 "Horizon" is our first stable release focused on user freedom and geographic data sovereignty.
